🔍 THE BOARDROOM AUTOPSY: Exposing the Host-Producer Grift in French Public Television

To find the real power brokers in French public television, do not look at the politicians on screen — look at the contracts hidden in the data room. The gap between the public mandate and the private invoice is exactly where the crime occurs.

In this explosive investigation, we perform a forensic autopsy on the “animateur-producteur” grift: the system where television hosts sell their own shows back to their own public networks, extracting staggering profit margins of up to 70%. 

We trace the money, follow the paper trail, and expose the revolving door between public executives and private production firms — the same executives who approve the contracts… then immediately join the companies that win them.

Most shocking of all: just TWO private societies now control 80% of public debate and opinion shows on France 5. 

Using a gritty retro-print noir aesthetic, we dissect contracts, whistleblower testimonies, and corporate org charts to reveal the hidden boardroom connections that shape what the French public sees — and doesn’t see.
