### Episode brief (from Spotify RSS — delete this section when done)

How did a teenage romance turn into one of France's most enigmatic criminal cases?In this first episode of our podcast series, we retrace the early days of Delphine and Cédric Jubillar's relationship. Delphine was a discreet 17-year-old nursing student when she met Cédric, a rebellious apprentice painter.Despite their differences and Cédric's "bad boy" image, the couple moved in together, bought an unfinished house in Cagnac-les-Mines, and started a family.From their modest wedding to their surface-level happy family life shared on social media, we explore how their shared childhood traumas bonded them together before their lives ultimately spiraled into tragedy.
