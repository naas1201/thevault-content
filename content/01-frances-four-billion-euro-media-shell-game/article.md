Four billion euros of public money vanishes every year into a black box — yet France’s official books claim perfect financial health.
In this dark investigative whiteboard exposé, we perform a forensic premortem on the financial architecture of the French public broadcasting system. Follow the red thread from captive public funding straight to the biggest winners: Mediawan and Banijay, who have captured hundreds of millions in guaranteed production contracts with virtually zero competitive risk.

Is the success of these private super-producers really the result of genius… or simply the massive luck of state-guaranteed funding and zero downside?
